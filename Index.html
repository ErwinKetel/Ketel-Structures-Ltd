Our Policies

Commitment to safety, sustainability, and ethical business practices

Health & Safety Policy

Ketel Structures Ltd. is dedicated to providing safe working conditions for all employees, contractors, and the surrounding community by effectively managing health and safety risks associated with our operations.

The company will:

  • We require all organizational leaders to demonstrate competency and accountability in health and safety matters, maintaining a highly visible commitment.
  • Establish, implement and review measurable health and safety objectives and targets to ensure continual improvement in our practices, procedures and performance.
  • Our goal is to eliminate work-related injuries and illnesses by proactively identifying and mitigating workplace hazards, continuously monitoring employee health, and implementing comprehensive training programs to ensure a safe and healthy work environment.
  • Comply with relevant health and safety legislation regulations and policies in our area of operation.
  • Accurately report, record, and investigate all workplace incidents and injuries, ensuring lessons are learned to prevent future occurrences.
  • Make our staff aware of their individual responsibilities to take reasonable care for the safety of themselves and others and to cooperate with the management in matters of safety.

All our staff members play a crucial role in achieving the policy aims and objectives by:

  • Identifying and reporting workplace hazards promptly.
  • Following established safety protocols and procedures.
  • Participating in health and safety training programs.
  • Contributing to a culture of continuous improvement in workplace safety.
  • Encouraging open communication and collaboration on safety matters.
  • Considering other people's health and safety.
  • Communicating effectively and cooperating with the management.

Our contractors will be required to adhere to the same high standards, ensuring that their health and safety policies are upheld and not compromised.

This policy will be actively monitored, regularly reviewed, and revised as necessary to maintain its effectiveness and relevance.

Environmental Policy

Ketel Structures Ltd. is committed to minimizing the environmental impact of our operations and promoting sustainable practices throughout our organization.

We are dedicated to:

  • Complying with all relevant environmental legislation, regulations, and other requirements.
  • Minimizing waste through careful planning and efficient use of materials and resources.
  • Reducing pollution and emissions through the implementation of appropriate controls and technologies.
  • Conserving energy and water through efficient operational practices and the use of renewable resources where feasible.
  • Promoting environmental awareness among our employees, suppliers, and clients.
  • Setting and reviewing environmental objectives and targets to ensure continuous improvement.
  • Incorporating environmental considerations into our business decisions and planning processes.
  • Using environmentally responsible products and materials in our projects whenever possible.
  • Protecting natural habitats and biodiversity in areas where we operate.
  • Responsibly managing the entire lifecycle of our projects from design through to decommissioning.

We will regularly monitor and review our environmental performance and communicate our progress to stakeholders. All employees are responsible for implementing this policy and are encouraged to suggest improvements to our environmental performance.

This policy will be reviewed annually to ensure its continuing suitability and effectiveness.
